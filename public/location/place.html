Tarkarli Beach

About Tarkarli Beach

Tarkarli Beach is a serene and beautiful destination located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra. Known for its pristine waters and white sandy shores, it is an ideal spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The beach is also famous for water sports like snorkeling, scuba diving, and parasailing. Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Tarkarli Beach is from October to March when the weather is pleasant, and the sea is calm. This period is perfect for enjoying water sports and exploring the natural beauty of the area.

Accessibility

This is easily accessible by road from major cities in Maharashtra. The nearest railway station is Kudal, located about 45 km away, and the nearest airport is Dabolim Airport, about 130 km away. Local buses, taxis, and private vehicles can be used to reach the beach from these locations.

Budget

Rs.7000

Routes

  • From Mumbai: Take the NH66 towards Malvan, then follow the signs to Tarkarli Beach. The journey is approximately 490 km and takes around 9 hours by road.
  • From Pune: Take the NH66 towards Malvan. The journey is around 400 km and takes about 8 hours by road.
  • From Goa: Take the NH66 north towards Malvan. Tarkarli Beach is about 130 km from Goa, taking approximately 3-4 hours by road.

Location

Tarkarli Beach, Sindhudurg, Maharashtra, India
